### Responsibilities

We are looking for a highly motivated PhD student in the areas AI4Science, Probabilistic Machine Learning, Bayesian Techniques/Optimization and Causal Modeling. The successful candidate will contribute to the prestigious doc.funds project BiotechPredict, an excellence program funded by the Austrian Science Fund. They will work within a vibrant and interdisciplinary team of researchers in AI and Biotechnology and apply AI techniques to the development of new enzymes and process optimization:

https://www.nawigraz.at/de/neuigkeiten/neu-doc.funds-biotechpredict-mit-nawi-graz-konsortium/

BiotechPredict invites 10 PhD candidates in the field of Biotechnology, Molecular Biology, and Computer Science aiming to create a leading hub for the application of Artificial Intelligence in Biotechnology. It joins the expertise of researchers from the Graz University of Technology in biotechnology, bioinformatics, process engineering, and computer science to break new ground in the prediction of catalytic properties of enzymes and understanding complex interactions in bioprocesses. 

### Tasks

Research (90%)

- research in Probabilistic Machine Learning, Bayesian Techniques/Optimization, Causal Modeling for Biotechnology and Enzyme Design
- publishing papers in leading AI/ML/Biotechnology conferences and journals
- working towards a PhD thesis in AI/ML or Biotechnology
- collaboration with project partners on Biotechnology and Enzyme Design
- supervision of Bachelor and Master projects
Administrative Duties (10%)

- organizational and administrative duties, project management, project meetings
Teaching (optional)

- if desired, the successful candidate can contribute to teaching activities in the group (e.g., machine learning, probabilistic machine learning)
