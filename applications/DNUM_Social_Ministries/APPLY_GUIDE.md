# 🚀 Application Guide: DNUM des Ministères Sociaux

## 📌 Summary
*   **Target:** Data Scientist (Alternance / Apprenticeship)
*   **Entity:** DNUM (Social Ministries) - DATA-IA Department.
*   **Location:** Montrouge (Paris Area) + Remote.
*   **Key Tech:** Python (Pandas, PyTorch), SQL, LLMs/Generative AI.
*   **Deadline:** Apply as soon as possible.

## 📄 Application Steps
1.  **Portal:** Click "Postuler" on the recruitment platform where you found the offer.
2.  **Documents:**
    *   ✅ **CV (French):** `resume_fr_EDT.pdf` (or any formal French resume).
    *   ✅ **Cover Letter (French):** `cover_letter_fr_DNUM.pdf`.
    *   ✅ **Transcripts:** (Bachelor + current Master/Engineering year).

## 💡 Strategic Advice
*   **The "Alternance" status**: If they ask about your student status for 2026, explain that you are graduating in July 2026 and are looking for a first professional experience. If they insist on the "Alternance" format, ask if they have **"Contrat de Projet"** or **"CDD"** openings for young graduates.
*   **Public Interest**: In the interview, emphasize why you care about the **Ministry of Health** or **Social Protection**. Show that you are not just a "coder" but a citizen who wants to help.
*   **LLMs**: Talk about your knowledge of **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**. The DNUM is currently building chatbots for public information—this is a perfect fit for your NLP background.
