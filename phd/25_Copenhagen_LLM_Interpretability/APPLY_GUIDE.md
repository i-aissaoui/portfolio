## 💡 Interview Tip
If you get an interview, they will ask: *"Why Mamba for interpretability?"*
**Your Answer**: *"Because Mamba uses a selective state space. By probing the 'selection' mechanism, we can understand exactly which parts of a sentence the model 'decides' to remember or forget. This is a much cleaner signal than the messy attention maps of a 70B parameter Transformer."*
